HOW TO BUY

Click the BUY NOW button above and connect your wallet. Once connected, you’ll see three options to buy LHINU tokens.

1. Buy LHINU with ETH/BNB

To buy tokens with ETH/BNB, enter how much LHINU you want to buy, or how much ETH/BNB you want to swap, then confirm the gas cost and the transaction in your wallet.

2. Buy LHINU with USDT

To buy tokens with USDT, enter how much LHINU you want to buy, or how much USDT you want to swap, approve the use of USDT in your wallet, then confirm the gas cost (in ETH) and the final transaction.

FAQS

What is Love Hate Inu all about?

Love Hate Inu is a meme coin with public voting utility verified by blockchain technology. Participants vote on issues one way or the other, thus minting new memes, and are rewarded in LHINU tokens.

Why am I voting on Love Hate Inu?

Things happen in the world every week that divide opinion. Social media is full of trolls, paid actors, and bots. This is a place to immutably decide on issues and mint memes of the results.

Why do I need LHINU tokens?

LHINU is the voting token of the Love Hate Inu platform. The more tokens you stake, the greater your voting power and influence over poll results. LHINU is also needed to create new polls.

Who creates the polls?

We will create the first few polls on Love Hate Inu but the ultimate goal is to have the community decide on what they want to see, and have the power to create new topics.

Is it possible to manipulate results?

Voting is only possible for stakers of LHINU tokens. All votes are recorded onto the blockchain via smart contracts and are immutable, thus ensuring the veracity of the results.
